MY JOURNEY

A lot of people have different motivation and purpose to change themselves as one might want to look big just to impress someone or just to be strong. But, Initially, when I decided to change, my motivation was just to prove everyone who taunted me that I can change too. I just wanted to change (No, I never thought of getting six-pack abs).

So, I was this guy who was quite unhealthy as you can see.
I was extremely overweight as I was in above 90kgs in this picture.

I was frustrated with being fat and constant taunts from all except my parents(because obviously, they do love you no matter how you look). So, at last, I decided to change myself and I was completely aimless as I just wanted to lose fat.

My school used to finish at 2:30 pm and at 4 pm I used to go to tuitions and guess what? I started going for running in a park near my tuition before my classes begin just after having a very small lunch (as I thought eating less is the only way to achieve this). This was my hectic schedule but still, it was of no use!
Then I bought this for my home:

Yes! A treadmill.
But let me tell you even though I worked out daily on this machine I never got any significant results. So, at last, I joined a gym where I worked out for 3 months and then left the gym. After 1 year I again joined the gym with unbreakable will power!

Started hitting weights and the results were slow but definitely, it didn’t demotivate me. I used to leave for college at 6:30 am with all my meals in my backpack and I used to come back home around 5:30 pm from where I used to go gym directly without any rest! When all of my friends were relaxing I was working hard.

Many people demotivated me in my path and because of that, I started becoming more stronger towards my goal. That year was the worst test of my mental and physical ability. But at last, I got what I wanted from so many years i.e I was lean now But wait!
After I was lean I again gained back what I had lost just because of my habits.
So I was again fat!

Now since I already knew what I have to do to lose fat I again lost the same amount of fat again in 3 months! Yes, just 3 months! I was at my lowest weight ever!

Now I had experience as well as knowledge so I started my bulk again with the aim of getting a good amount of muscles. At last, I was able to get a good amount of muscles on my body and strength.

Obviously, I now have different and much bigger goals for my physique as I want to keep on improving myself. I sincerely thank all those who never left me and kept me motivated throughout the journey.

I don’t do any cheat meal other than on my birthdays or vacation and that adds to maybe twice or thrice a year! Even I don’t know how I changed myself so much.

But due to this journey, I realized I have a much bigger goal than just improving myself and i.e to help people change themselves through my experiences. (those who have been through this knows what it takes to achieve something that not much people try to think of).

I thought to get in and out information on how our body and nutrition works which can definitely compliment my experience of real-life training. Thus, I got myself internationally accredited in fitness and nutrition. I am an advance diploma holder in fitness and diploma holder in nutrition.

Many people go to the gym and eventually give up on their goals due to lack of guidance and knowledge. I always feel blessed when someone approaches me for personal coaching.

I don’t work like most of the personal trainers or coaches who just provides software generated plans and the false hope of miraculous transformation within a small period of time with an aim to get huge profits. Not all people can afford such expensive online coaching programs thus I provide one that is not compromised on the quality and hard work put into making each of it.

It is the one that almost all people can afford and not let money be the reason to not change. Remember when you will change yourself I will be the happiest guy after you.
